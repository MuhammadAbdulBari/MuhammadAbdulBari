## About Me

I'm a software engineering student and developer based in **Karachi, Pakistan**, building web and mobile applications. I care more about whether something *actually works* than whether it just looks good on a landing page.

INTP by nature — I spend a lot of time taking problems apart to understand how they really function, then building systems around that understanding. Most of what I do sits at the intersection of **web development, mobile apps, and backend engineering**.
